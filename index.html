Welcome to Design Fix Ltd

At DESIGN FIX LTD we work with clients to create aesthetically pleasing rooms and spaces. Clients range from homeowners to large corporations, and the spaces designed are equally varied, ranging from simple indoor and outdoor home environments to Offices, Hotel lobbies and lavish mansions.
